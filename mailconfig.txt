When Contact Pruning is enabled, contacts are automatically blocked after a specified number of emails are sent with 0 opens.
You can change the status back to "active".
a) Turn Contact Pruning off.
https://elasticemail.com/account#/settings/new/tracking-and-pruning
b) From Contacts click on All Contacts and then on Status. Tick the Stale box and on the new display click on the Email blue down arrow and choose Select All.
Click on the More Options drop down menu and select Change Status, just follow the directions.
Switch the contact’s status to “active”.
c) Finally turn on contact pruning on again. The contact should remain with active status.
