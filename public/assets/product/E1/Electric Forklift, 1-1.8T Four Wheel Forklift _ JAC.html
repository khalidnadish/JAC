Selling Point

1. The electric forklift of H series employs asymmetric drive axle, to leave enough space for the installation of the travel motor. So, the motor replacement and maintenance can be completed easily, without troublesome dismantling of the gear box and drive axle. This design has been granted the national patent.

2. The steering axle of the 1-1.8T four wheel forklift offers big turning angle to reduce the turning radius and enhance the trafficability of the electric forklift. The patented technology makes this product quite popular.

3. Iron cover for the balance weight of the electric forklift has high strength and good resistance to crush.

4. Thanks to the LCD instrument panel with good resistance to water and vibration from CURTIS, the important information can be displayed on the screen completely, enabling the user to understand the working conditions of the material handling equipment timely and precisely.

5. This range of product is installed with CURTIS accelerator. According to ergonomics, the foot brake pedal is designed slightly higher than the accelerator.

6. Imported AMP connector to ensure high reliability of the electrical circuit.

7. Making full use of space, we maximize the size of the fins to achieve optimal heat dissipation effect.
