Selling Point

1. The air intake system installed at high level, as well as the two air filters of the warehouse equipment, offers full protection to the engine of the diesel forklift.

2. The drum brake and the vacuum servo enable convenient and sensitive car braking.

3. Smartly designed, the V shape engine mount of the diesel forklift effectively reduces the vibration during operation and creates comfortable working environment for the operator.

4. To extend the lifespan of the bearing and the kingpin, oilless bearing is adopted for self-lubrication.

5. The tailstock of the material handling equipment adopts integrated design to enhance the shock resistance performance of the product. The technology has been granted the national patent.

6. Our patented technologies-10T main reducing gear and the axle housing effectively improve the bearing capacity of the drive axle.

7. Since the fork arm carrier of the container forklift truck is installed with 6 idler wheels, the diesel forklift is quite suitable for high-strength working conditions.

8. Diaphragm type clutch pressure plate and the patented shift fork enable convenient operation of the clutch.
