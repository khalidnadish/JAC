Features

1. High strength truck body ensures high durability.

2. Maximum gradeability reaches up to 20% under full load.

3. Maximum travel speed can be 20km/h under full load.

4. The parameters such as travel speed, lift speed can be freely adjusted.

5. Lift speed: 340mm/s (standard type), 420mm/s (power type)

6. Charging time: 90-110 minutes (High power on-board charger can be optionally equipped to realize convenient charging.)

7. Working environment temperature: -20-60°C

8. The eco-friendly design of the diesel forklift offers full protection to both the user and the environment.
9. Small overall dimension, low centre of gravity, small turning radius, and excellent manoeuvrability.
10. The frame and the steering axle of the material handling equipment are engineered with shock absorber to reduce the impact and maximize the comfort during operation.
11. Multiple engines for selection.
