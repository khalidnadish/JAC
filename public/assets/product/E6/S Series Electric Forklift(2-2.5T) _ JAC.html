CPD15S/CPD18S electric forklifts have high transmission efficiency and low noise due to their integrated drive axle and gearbox structure. These electric forklifts feature low energy consumption and a long working time. In addition, they have power regeneration function to prolong the service time which recovers the electric quantity of battery when the electric forklift is braking, slowing down or steering. Their energy-saving design will automatically cut off the power supply of the motor when the forklift is stopping for more than 5 seconds. These electric forklifts are equipped with shock absorber steering axle, two rearview mirrors, and suspension OPS seat to create a comfortable and safe working environment for the driver.

Selling Point

1. The integrated drive axle and gearbox structure ensures high transmission efficiency and low noise.

2. Power regeneration function prolongs the service time that it recovers the electric quantity of battery when the electric forklift is braking, slowing down or steering.

3. Channel steel mast is strong and reliable. Its small front suspension provides convenience for loading and unloading goods.

4. The shock absorber steering axle greatly reduces the vibration during the driving.

5. The fast, medium, and slow drive modes provides different speeds for various workspace and working distances.

6. Curtis LCD instrument displays detailed vehicle information and enables convenient operation.

7. The suspension OPS seat with free back and forth movement and adjustable backrest angle provides a comfortable and stable working environment for the driver.
