Features

1. The hydraulic drive system of H series diesel forklift provides stable and efficient driving effect.

2. The hyperbolic design for the main reducer of drive axle helps realize smooth meshing. Apart from the low noise, this design extends the lifespan of the drive axle, as well.

3. To improve the working environment, we specially manufacture the hood of the diesel powered forklift with heat-insulated and sound absorbing material. Apart from that, the gap is filled with rubber for vibration absorption.

4. Our diesel forklift is meticulously designed according to ergonomics, which effectively enlarges the working space and minimizes the fatigue of the operator.

5. Thanks to the drum brake, the sensitive braking effect of the heavy-duty forklift truck makes this product particularly safe and reliable for use.

6. The fuel tank cap is located at the side of the tank. The self-locking structure of the cap completely avoids fuel leakage.

7. The simple and attractive structure of the overhead guard makes the dismantling of the product much easier. In the meantime, the impact resistance of the lift truck is greatly improved.

8. The oil pump speed controller is upgraded to reduce blue smoke generated during engine idling and cold start.
