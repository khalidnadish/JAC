Features

1. Neutral gear safety system- Prevent the accidents that caused by accidential starting.

2. The combined type design of the taillight makes for nice appearance of the 1-1.8T four wheel forklift.

3. The imported CURTIS controller is installed with our patented technology, to achieve optimal heat dissipation effect.

4. The transverse gearbox of the electric forklift effectively improves the transmission efficiency and working hours of the battery.

5. Granted the national patent, the 360° rotatable plate-type drive axle makes the maintenance of the motor and the gearbox easier.

6. Our industrial forklift is installed with CURTIS accelerator. Based on ergonomics, we particularly manufacture the electric forklift with the foot brake pedal slightly higher than the accelerator.

7. REMA connector for convenient power supply connection

8. The lift truck is constructed with LCD instrument panel with good resistance to water and vibration from CURTIS. The important information can be displayed on the screen completely. So the user can understand the working conditions of the product timely and precisely.
